What is a Popped Water Lily Seed?

Popped Water Lily Seeds come from the Euryale ferox plant found in the still ponds of Eastern India. These seeds have been sun-dried, roasted, and hand-popped into a light, crunchy snack rooted in Ayurveda for centuries.

We’ve given this ancient superfood a modern twist—roasted in olive oil for real flavor and a satisfying crunch. Naturally gluten-free, plant-based, and nutrient-rich, they’re a wholesome snack that’s as delicious as it is good for you.
